COMMERCIAL_BU(Commercial Business Unit metadata)
Commercial_BU_Name: Name of the commercial business unit. Sample values: [BU1, BU2, BU3]
Commercial_BU: Code of the commercial business unit. Sample values: [CBU01, CBU02]

SALES_DATA(Sales transactional data)
Client_Partner: Name of the client partner handling the account. Sample values: [John Smith, Sarah Johnson]
Opportunity_Owner: Name of the opportunity owner. Sample values: [Manish Nair, Madhu Narasimhan]
Department: Department associated with the sale. Sample values: [Integration, Consulting]
Account_Name: Name of the account/customer. Sample values: [Boston Scientific, SAP Oracle]
Amount: Total deal amount. Sample values: [100000, 500000]
Close_Date: Date when the deal was closed. Sample values: [2023-01-15, 2023-05-20]
